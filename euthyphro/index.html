    Euthyphro: Personal Wiki

    • .ZIP compressed
    • Open in browser (manually save the HTML file)

    This software enables you to maintain a personal wiki of Plato’s Euthyphro. Appropriate for both independent research and coursework, this personal wiki lets you keep a custom-annotated version of Euthyphro for lifelong study tailored to your educational needs. David Fisco provides this wiki to you free of charge under an open source license.

    This wiki offers the Benjamin Jowett translation of Plato’s Euthyphro.

    You will need to learn how to use TiddlyWiki, the software on which the Euthyphro wiki is based. (TiddlyWiki isn’t too difficult to master.) Since TiddlyWiki runs in most modern browsers, you can use your Web browser to view and edit your personal Euthyphro wiki, even when offline. Tablets and smartphones are also supported.

    You can take a peek at the Euthyphro wiki without downloading it to your machine.

    Learning TiddlyWiki

    TiddlyWiki is the software underlying the Euthyphro wiki. You must learn the basic administration and editing of TiddlyWiki to maximize use of your wiki. I recommend using these resources:

    1. Introduction to TiddlyWiki
      A YouTube video from TiddlyWiki’s creator, Jeremy Ruston, giving you an overview of TiddlyWiki
    2. Discover TiddlyWiki
      A bit more in-depth introduction to the features of TiddlyWiki
    3. Learning TiddlyWiki
      A collection of tiddlers you can use to learn administration and use of TiddlyWiki
    4. WikiText
      Information on the markup language you will use to edit tiddlers. WikiText resembles Markdown.

    Download your Euthyphro wiki

    • .ZIP compressed
      Download a compressed version of the wiki. If not already on your computer, you will need .ZIP software to decompress the file.
    • HTML in browser
      Open the wiki in a new window or tab in your browser. You will need to use the “Save as…” functionality of your browser to save the HTML file to your desired location in your file system.

    Release information

    The current version of the Euthyphro wiki was released on 19 February 2015. Corrections and additions to the base Euthyphro wiki will be posted on this site.

    Upgrading TiddlyWiki

    You will probably want to upgrade the underlying TiddlyWiki software for your Euthyphro wiki. You can upgrade by following the TiddlyWiki upgrade instructions.
